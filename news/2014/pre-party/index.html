2014 Pre Party

Posted June 20 2014 by Robbie Clutton

Better late then never, we'd like to announce the GORUCO pre-party to take place at the offices of Pivotal Labs.

Please join us from 7pm - 9pm at the new home of Pivotal Labs, 2nd floor, 625 Avenue of the Americas (6th Avenue to you and me), between 18th and 19th streets.

Please feel free to pass around this invite to friends in the Ruby community.
